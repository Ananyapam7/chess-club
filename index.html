About

A hub for chess players of IISER-K to learn, play, and improve together.

LEARN

We welcome people with a wide range of skills and experience, from beginners to advanced.

PRACTICE

We hold sessions on weekends, where we discuss strategies, learn openings and endgames, solve puzzles, and play games. Practice makes perfect!

IMPROVE

Chess is best learnt together, where we can pool our knowledge. Join us as we hone our chess skills and critical thinking!

Events


Institute Events


These are some of the most celebrated and illustrious events hosted by Chess Club of IISER-K




Grand Chess Tournament

The most commemorated chess tournament at IISER-K, with participants from the entire institute, battling for a position against the best of the players, to eventually represent the institute to compete at the IISM.


Format: Classical

System: Round Robin

Rating Matches

Rating Matches continue to be the most popular event of all time. Players with similar ratings are matched together as they play rapid games in a swiss system.


Format: Rapid

System: Swiss

Inter-Batch Series

The Inter-Batch tournament series quite fancies the students from the institute. Students of a batch come together to contend against other batches to claim the title of winners.


Format: Rapid

System: Single-Elimination

Blitz Chess

Introduced recently, blitz chess creates a flurry of moves, as both players attempt to hustle to victory. Quite enjoyed by folks who love to experience a blazing fast game and even faster thinking.


Format: Blitz

System: Swiss
