About

A hub for chess players of IISER-K to learn, play, and improve together.

LEARN

We welcome people with a wide range of skills and experience, from beginners to advanced.

PRACTICE

We hold sessions on weekends, where we discuss strategies, learn openings and endgames, solve puzzles, and play games. Practice makes perfect!

IMPROVE

Chess is best learnt together, where we can pool our knowledge. Join us as we hone our chess skills and critical thinking!
