Welcome to the chess club of IISER Kolkata

About

The foremost society in campus dedicated to chess

A hub for chess players of IISER-K to learn, play, and improve together.
We welcome people with a wide range of skills and experience in the game, from beginners to advanced. Every weekend we come together and discuss different strategies, learn new openings and solve extremely critical puzzles. Join us as we hone our skills chess and critical thinking!

IMPROVE
